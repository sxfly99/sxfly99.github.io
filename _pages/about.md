<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

I am a Ph.D. student (2023) at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University, supervised by <a href="https://web.xidian.edu.cn/ldli/index.html">Prof. Leida Li</a>. Prior to this, I received the B.Sc. degree from China University of Mining and Technology. My research interests include visual quality assessment and computational aesthetics. I serve as a reviewer for journals and conferences including TIP, PR, CVPR, AAAI, ACM MM, ECCV, etc.
